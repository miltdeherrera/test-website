Steely Dan's Best Albums

Here are the five best albums by Steely Dan, in no particular order.

  • Gaucho: their 1980 masterpiece about the beknighted, sand-dusted city of Los Angeles.
  • Aja: So sad. So beautiful.
  • The Royal Scam: We can still have fun with it.
  • Katy Lied: Just the prescription... as ordered up by Doctor Wu.
  • The Nightfly: Fagen solo masterpiece sneaks onto the list like one of those dastardly Reds!
