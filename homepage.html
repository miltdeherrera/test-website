Steely Dan

The best band in the world

Steely Dan was formed in the 1960s. Their sardonic style of rock has retained influence and importance among a small core of fans for the past five decades.

Innovation throughout the years

From jazz rock and blues licks to disco and new wave, Steely Dan did it all. (Assuming that you are allowed to use solo work by either principal to supplement this definition of "doing it all.")

Current status

Walter Becker died on September 3, 2017. Donald Fagen still tours live.
